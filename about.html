Know About Us

Affinity Overseas Trust Bank(Affinity) provides economic and financial intelligence for the world's financial sector and has built a reputation for objective and incisive reporting.
Affinity Overseas Trust Bankhas been the trusted source of banking information since 2003. Affinity has navigated its way through over 18 years of banking coverage from the Wall Street Crash of 2006 to the financial crisis of 2008 with the same reputation for accuracy, authority and integrity.
Affinity Overseas Trust Bankstrives to stimulate and inform, offering an unparalleled outlook with a wide range of news, features, analysis and interviews, and in-depth coverage of all the key issues facing the financial sector.
Affinity Overseas Trust Bankcombines in-depth regional and country coverage with reports on:
Markets: Capital markets, derivatives & structured products, infrastructure & project finance and Islamic finance.
Banking, regulation and risk: Regulation, retail banking, wholesale banking, private banking, management and governance issues
Transactions and technology: trading, trade finance, securities services, cash management and transaction bankers.
Data: International financial centres, trends and rankings, including the Top 1000 World Banks
Affinity Overseas Trust Bankoffers an unrivalled combination of authoritative news, features, surveys and heavyweight interviews and is essential reading for CEOs, CFOs, corporate treasurers, central bank governors and financial regulators. Its unique database of more than 100 banks maps their financial strength and soundness via Tier 1 capital, their profitability and their performance versus their peers.
Affinity Overseas Trust Bank is the key source of data and analysis for the industry.

We’re changing the way people feel about banking.
Affinity Overseas Trust Bank business was built on the principle of protecting our members and helping them get ahead. That means we don’t profit off of you. We profit with you: every time you use your Affinity Overseas Trust debit card, we earn a small amount from Visa (paid by the merchant).

Our Culture

We believe that creating a company where people love to work is built on relationships, growth, and impact. We cultivate a culture of low ego and set a high bar for excellence. We believe in flat organizations and open work spaces. We treat one another inside the company the same way we treat our members–by communicating in ways that build trust, operating with transparency, and having one another’s back.
We recognize the contributions of individuals and celebrate wins as a team. Every member of the team is an owner of the company and is encouraged to “Affinity” on any part of the business. What we all have in common is a mission that starts with heart, and a desire to work together to create something of lasting value and personal impact for our employees and members alike.
